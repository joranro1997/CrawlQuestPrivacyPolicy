CrawlQuest Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 18, 2025

1. Introduction

CrawlQuest ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1. Information You Provide

  • Username: The name you choose to participate in sessions
  • Profile Image: Optional, if you choose to upload a profile photo
  • Language Preferences: The language you select in the application

2.2. Automatically Collected Information

  • Location: Your GPS location in real-time when participating in an active session
  • Session Data: Information about sessions you participate in (session code, visited stops, score)
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app (features used, frequency of use)
  • Device Identifiers: Unique identifiers associated with your device

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Generate personalized routes based on your location
  • Enable multiplayer functionality and share information with other session participants
  • Display your real-time location to other participants in the same session
  • Manage scores and rankings within sessions
  • Personalize your experience (language, preferences)
  • Display relevant advertising (through Google AdMob)
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Sharing Information

4.1. With Other Users

When you participate in a session, the following information is visible to other participants:

  • Your username
  • Your profile image (if you have uploaded one)
  • Your real-time location during the active session
  • Your score and statistics within the session

4.2. With Service Providers

We share information with:

  • Supabase: To store session data, participants, and locations
  • Google Maps/Places API: To generate routes and find nearby bars
  • Google AdMob: To display personalized advertising

4.3. We Do Not Sell Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or market your personal information to third parties for their own marketing purposes.

5. Data Storage

  • Local Data: Your username, profile image, and preferences are stored locally on your device
  • Cloud Data: Session data (codes, participants, routes, scores) is stored on Supabase servers
  • Location: Your location is only shared during active sessions and is deleted when the session ends

6. Data Retention

  • Completed session data is retained to allow session history
  • You can delete your session history from the application
  • Real-time location data is deleted when the session ends

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectify: Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete: Request deletion of your data
  • Object: Object to the processing of your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format

To exercise these rights, contact us at: midwayofdraenor@gmail.com

8. Security

We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

9. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertising. AdMob may collect information about your device and activity to personalize ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings.

For more information about how Google uses data, visit: Google Privacy Policy

10. Minors

CrawlQuest is intended for users over 18 years of age due to its alcohol-related content. We do not knowingly collect information from minors under 18 years of age. If we discover that we have collected information from a minor, we will delete it immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

12. Consent

By using CrawlQuest, you consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to our Terms of Use.

13. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us:

Email: midwayofdraenor@gmail.com

14. Legal Basis (GDPR)

For users in the European Union, we process your personal data based on:

  • Consent: For the use of location and participation in sessions
  • Contract Performance: To provide the application services
  • Legitimate Interest: To improve our services and display relevant advertising
